I am a Ph.D. Candidate in Astronomy at **Shanghai Jiao Tong University**.

My research focuses on cosmology and galaxy evolution, with interests in both numerical simulations and observational constraints.

## Research Interests

- Cosmological simulations: AGN and stellar feedback, hydrodynamic solvers
- Observational cosmology: halo boundary and large-scale structure
- Galaxy evolution: galaxy-halo connection and galaxy quenching

## Research Experience

- Research Assistant, Department of Astronomy, Shanghai Jiao Tong University (Aug 2020 - Aug 2021)
- Research Intern, University of Victoria, Canada (Jul 2019 - Aug 2019)
- Undergraduate Research Assistant, Nanjing University (Oct 2017 - Dec 2019)
